About the Monastery

A House of Prayer in Northern California

Rooted in Ancient Faith

Gishen Mariam Monastery & Church stands as a sacred sanctuary in the hills of Shasta County, carrying forward the unbroken witness of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church.

For over seventeen centuries, our tradition has preserved the apostolic faith through prayer, fasting, sacred liturgy, and monastic life. Here in California, we continue that legacy — offering a spiritual home for the Ethiopian community and all who seek the living God.

Our clergy and monastics dedicate their lives to worship, intercession, and service. Through Divine Liturgy, the holy sacraments, and acts of mercy, we strive to grow in holiness and serve our neighbors with the love of Christ.
